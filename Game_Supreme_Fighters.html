Supreme Fighters
Made by: Developer Bosko Ivkovic & Artist Moses Decaster

Supreme Fighters is an exhilarating 2.5D beat 'em up online fighting game that brings the thrill of classic arcade combat into a modern multiplayer arena.

Up to four players can clash in intense battles, whether in team-based matchups or chaotic free-for-alls. The game offers a variety of modes, including the strategic 'Streets' mode and the relentless 'Survival' mode, ensuring that every match feels fresh and exciting.
Each character in Supreme Fighters comes with a unique moveset and an ultimate ability, allowing players to master different playstyles and pull off game-changing moves that can completely alter the course of the fight.
