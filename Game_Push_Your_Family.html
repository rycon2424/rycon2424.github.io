Push Your Family
Made by: Meteor Mug, my role: Lead Network Developer

Push Your Family is a hilariously chaotic multiplayer party game featuring wacky physics that guarantees fun for everyone, whether you're a seasoned gamer or just picking up a controller for the first time. The game's intuitive design makes it easy for anyone to jump right in—no tutorial required—so even your mom can join in on the laughter-filled mayhem.
With a variety of entertaining game modes to choose from, Push Your Family is the perfect pick for spontaneous get-togethers and family game nights.
