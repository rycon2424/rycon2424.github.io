Spy x Atms
Made by: Bosko Ivkovic

Spy X ATMs is a third person online stealth game where different players are against eachother in hacking atm's for money and delivering them to drop of points. You play as a spy and have to acquire a disguise by murdering wandering npc civilians.

You can only achieve your goal if you deliver the money in stealth. Other players will try to do the same or if they spot you in your disguise they can shoot you to temporarily disable you and steal your money.
