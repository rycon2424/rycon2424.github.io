Puzzle Masters

Puzzle Masters was a school project where I had to create and design a game during covid that would be fun to play and make people communicate with eachother so they get to know eachother better.

Puzzle masters is a multiplayer co-op puzzle game where you play with 4 players in total (with each their own unique abilities) and have to complete puzzles using eachothers strengths.

Day of Gladiators

Day Of Gladiators is a personal project inspired by the beloved Swords & Sandals series, where I took on the challenge of creating my own version with unique twists and design changes.

As a fun side project, I developed the game from the ground up, making all the armor and weapon art in photoshop myself. This project allowed me to dive deep into game development while putting my own creative spin on the classic Swords & Sandals combat experience.
