Lone Wolf
Made by: Bosko Ivkovic

Lone Wolf is a gripping first-person tactical shooter that challenges players to single-handedly clear levels filled with highly intelligent terrorists. Equipped with a primary weapon and a pistol of your choice, you'll need to rely on precision and strategy to survive.

The enemies in Lone Wolf feature advanced AI—they respond to sounds, react to discovering dead teammates, and coordinate their movements to flank and surround you. The game’s hardcore difficulty ensures that every encounter is a tense battle for survival, where a single headshot is lethal to both you and your enemies. Can you outsmart and outgun the opposition all on your own?
