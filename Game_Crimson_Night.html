Crimson Night
Made by: Bosko Ivkovic

Crimson Night is a dark medieval rpg with combat in a JRPG setting. Its a coop game where you can play with 1 to 4 players and there are 4 characters to create and control. There are 7 different classes with each their unique skills and talents.

During combat you have to be on edge because every action and being attacked required the player to perform a skillcheck. The accurate the skillcheck the better the result. This makes the game fair but also difficult. After combat the players come into a hub where they can equip their looted gear and expand the city and meet joining npcs that have all kinds of proffesions.
